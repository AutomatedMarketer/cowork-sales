# 6 contribution moves (give first)

1. **Share their work** — post their content to YOUR audience with a thoughtful caption
2. **Send referral** — connect them with someone who could buy from them
3. **Intro** — connect them with someone valuable (peer, mentor, potential collaborator)
4. **Thoughtful comment** — engage publicly on their best recent post (not "great post!")
5. **Send specific praise** — DM them ONE specific thing in their work that hit you
6. **Recommend a resource** — send them something useful unprompted (article, tool, intro to a process)

Pick ONE — quality > quantity. The contribution should take 5-15 minutes max.
