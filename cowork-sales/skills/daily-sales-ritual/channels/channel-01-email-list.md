# Channel 01 — Owned email list

## What it means
You own a list of people who gave you permission to email them. Unlike social platforms, you control the relationship — no algorithm stands between you and your reader.

## Daily activity gist
Daily or weekly send + reply to every inbound reply + Re-Reach-Out (RRO) sequence to silent openers

## Suggested tactics (auto-selected by /onboard-sales Phase 3)
1. Send one value email or story email every weekday; sell on Friday
2. Reply personally to every reply that comes in within 24 hours
3. Run a 3-email RRO sequence to anyone who opened but didn't click in the last 7 days

## Sample outbound prompt (rotated in /daily-sales-ritual)
> "Write today's email — one value lesson + one soft CTA to [offer] — max 300 words, plain text, no images."

## Buyer-pyramid signal patterns (Tool 4.2)
- **Buying now:** Clicked a pricing, sales page, or checkout link; replied asking "how do I sign up?" or "what's included?"
- **Open:** Opened 3+ emails this month but never clicked; replied with a question about the topic
- **Interested:** Opened occasionally; clicked a content link (blog, video) but not a sales link
- **Quiet but listening:** On the list 30+ days; opens fewer than 1 in 5 emails; never replied or clicked

## When this channel is wrong for the user
You have fewer than 50 subscribers — the channel exists but won't generate momentum until the list is larger.
