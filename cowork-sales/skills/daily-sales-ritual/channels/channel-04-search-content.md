# Channel 04 — Search & content (Google SEO / blog / YouTube SEO)

## What it means
You publish content optimized for search so buyers find you when they're actively looking for the solution you sell — compounding traffic that works while you sleep.

## Daily activity gist
Publish 1 SEO-optimized piece per week + respond to inbound discovery inquiries + update existing top-performing pages monthly

## Suggested tactics (auto-selected by /onboard-sales Phase 3)
1. Publish one long-form blog post or YouTube video per week targeting a keyword your buyer is actively searching (e.g., "how to [solve the problem you solve]")
2. Add a clear CTA at the end of every piece — one next step, one offer, or one opt-in
3. Reply personally to every comment, email, or DM that originates from a content piece within 24 hours

## Sample outbound prompt (rotated in /daily-sales-ritual)
> "Write the outline for this week's SEO post — target keyword: [keyword] — H1, 5-7 H2 sections, CTA at the end to [offer or opt-in]. Keep it under 200 words."

## Buyer-pyramid signal patterns (Tool 4.2)
- **Buying now:** Found your content via search and immediately emailed or DM'd asking about pricing or availability; went from a blog post directly to your sales page
- **Open:** Subscribed to your list after reading a post; left a comment asking a follow-up question; watched a YouTube video past the 70% mark
- **Interested:** Visited 3+ pages on your site in one session; bookmarked or shared your content; left a generic positive comment
- **Quiet but listening:** Organic traffic visit with no action; read the post and bounced; skipped to the end of a video

## When this channel is wrong for the user
You cannot commit to publishing at least one substantive piece per week — SEO is a long game that stalls without consistency.
