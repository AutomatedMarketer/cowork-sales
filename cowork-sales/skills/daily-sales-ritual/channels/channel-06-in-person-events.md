# Channel 06 — In-person & events

## What it means
You show up physically — at local meetups, industry conferences, networking events, or your own workshops — and convert real-world relationships into business.

## Daily activity gist
Attend or host 1 event per week or month + send follow-up messages to every meaningful connection within 48 hours

## Suggested tactics (auto-selected by /onboard-sales Phase 3)
1. Attend one relevant in-person event per week (local Chamber, industry meetup, masterminds, conferences) — or host your own small event (dinner, workshop, roundtable) once a month
2. Collect contact information for every meaningful conversation — business card, phone number, or LinkedIn connection before you leave
3. Send a personal follow-up within 48 hours referencing something specific from your conversation; don't pitch — continue the relationship

## Sample outbound prompt (rotated in /daily-sales-ritual)
> "Write 3 post-event follow-up messages for people I met at [event name]. Each should reference one thing we talked about, express genuine interest in continuing the conversation, and include one light next step (a resource, a question, or a suggested call). Max 4 sentences each."

## Buyer-pyramid signal patterns (Tool 4.2)
- **Buying now:** Asked at the event "do you take clients?" or "what would it cost to work together?"; followed up first after the event asking for a proposal
- **Open:** Replied warmly to your follow-up; agreed to a follow-up call; connected on LinkedIn and engaged with your post within a week
- **Interested:** Exchanged cards and chatted for 10+ minutes; replied to your follow-up with a friendly but non-committal response
- **Quiet but listening:** Accepted your LinkedIn connection but never responded to messages; took your card but never followed up

## When this channel is wrong for the user
There are no relevant in-person events in your area or industry — forcing attendance at unrelated events burns time with zero ROI.
