# Channel 02 — Social presence (FB / IG / LinkedIn / X / TikTok / YouTube)

## What it means
You publish content on one or two social platforms consistently and use that visibility to attract inbound interest, warm relationships, and direct sales conversations.

## Daily activity gist
Daily post + comment on 5-10 others' posts + DM anyone who engaged warmly

## Suggested tactics (auto-selected by /onboard-sales Phase 3)
1. Post one piece of original content per day (story, lesson, behind-the-scenes, or offer mention)
2. Leave 5-10 genuine, value-adding comments on posts from your target audience or peers
3. DM everyone who liked, commented, or shared your post and start a conversation — no pitch in the first message

## Sample outbound prompt (rotated in /daily-sales-ritual)
> "Write today's LinkedIn post — one contrarian lesson about [niche topic] — hook line, 3 short paragraphs, soft CTA to start a conversation in the comments."

## Buyer-pyramid signal patterns (Tool 4.2)
- **Buying now:** DM'd you asking for pricing, a link, or "how do I work with you?"; commented "I need this" or tagged someone else
- **Open:** Commented on 2+ of your recent posts; watched your Stories to the end; replied to a Story sticker poll
- **Interested:** Liked several posts in a row; followed you after one specific post; saved your post
- **Quiet but listening:** Follows you but never engages; views your profile occasionally (LinkedIn analytics); watches your videos without liking

## When this channel is wrong for the user
You genuinely hate posting and will abandon it within two weeks — consistency is the entire mechanism here.
