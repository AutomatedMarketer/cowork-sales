# Channel 05 — Direct cold outreach (cold email / DM / LinkedIn)

## What it means
You identify specific people who fit your ideal buyer profile and reach out to them directly — no prior relationship required — with a personalized, relevant first message.

## Daily activity gist
Send 10-20 personalized first-touch messages + 5-10 follow-ups to non-responders from earlier in the week

## Suggested tactics (auto-selected by /onboard-sales Phase 3)
1. Send 10-20 hyper-personalized first-touch messages per day — reference something specific to them (a post they wrote, a company milestone, a mutual challenge); one sentence on why you're reaching out; one low-friction ask (a question, not a pitch)
2. Follow up once, 3-4 days later, with a different angle — add value or ask a different question; never just "bumping this"
3. Move any positive reply into a real conversation — your goal is a call or a reply that opens a dialogue, not an immediate sale

## Sample outbound prompt (rotated in /daily-sales-ritual)
> "Write 3 cold outreach messages to [prospect type] on [platform]. Each should reference one specific thing about them, explain in one sentence why I'm reaching out, and end with one low-friction question. Max 5 sentences each."

## Buyer-pyramid signal patterns (Tool 4.2)
- **Buying now:** Replied asking for more details, a proposal, or pricing; booked a call directly from your first or second message
- **Open:** Replied with a question or requested more information; opened your LinkedIn message but hasn't responded yet (read receipt)
- **Interested:** Replied positively but deflected timing ("reach me next quarter"); connected on LinkedIn after your message
- **Quiet but listening:** Never replied; opened the email but took no action; viewed your LinkedIn profile after the message

## When this channel is wrong for the user
You don't have a defined prospect list or a clear buyer profile — cold outreach without targeting is spam.
