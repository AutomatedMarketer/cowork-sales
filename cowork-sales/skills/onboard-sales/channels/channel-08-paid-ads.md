# Channel 08 — Paid ads (FB / IG / Google / YouTube)

## What it means
You pay platforms to show your offer or content to precisely targeted audiences — buying attention instead of earning it organically.

## Daily activity gist
Manage 1-3 active campaigns + check key metrics daily + iterate creative or targeting weekly

## Suggested tactics (auto-selected by /onboard-sales Phase 3)
1. Run one lead-generation or direct-response campaign at a time until it's consistently profitable — master one before adding a second
2. Check your cost-per-lead, click-through rate, and cost-per-acquisition every morning; flag any metric that moves more than 20% in either direction
3. Rotate creative (ad image or video) every 7-14 days or when CTR drops below your baseline; kill ad sets spending more than 3x your target CPA without a conversion

## Sample outbound prompt (rotated in /daily-sales-ritual)
> "Review my ad metrics for today: CTR [X%], CPL $[X], CPA $[X], spend $[X]. Tell me what to pause, what to scale, and write one new ad hook to test."

## Buyer-pyramid signal patterns (Tool 4.2)
- **Buying now:** Clicked the ad and landed on the checkout or sales page; added to cart but didn't complete; visited the page 3+ times via retargeting
- **Open:** Clicked the ad and visited the offer page once; opted in to the lead magnet; engaged with the video ad (watched 75%+)
- **Interested:** Watched 25-50% of a video ad; clicked but bounced quickly; engaged with the ad post (like, comment, share)
- **Quiet but listening:** Seen the ad multiple times (high frequency) but never clicked; in the retargeting pool but no action

## When this channel is wrong for the user
Your monthly ad budget is below $300 — you won't generate enough data to optimize, and you'll burn through the budget before getting a meaningful signal.
