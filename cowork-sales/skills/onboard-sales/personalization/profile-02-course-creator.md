# Profile 02 — Course creator

## Who this fits
You sell knowledge — courses, memberships, cohorts, or digital programs — to students who want to learn a specific skill or achieve a specific transformation. Examples: online business course, photography masterclass, fitness program, professional certification prep, language learning cohort.

## Smart-suggestion output (Phase 2 of /onboard-sales)
The wizard says:
> "Based on your business-brain, I see you're a course creator. For someone in your shape, I'd suggest **Channel #01 (Owned email list)** + **Channel #02 (Social presence)**. But here are all 8 — pick 1-2 PRIMARY for YOU."

## Why these channels
Email is the engine of course launches — every successful course business uses email as the primary conversion channel. Your list is the audience you own; social platforms are rented land. Open launches, cart-close sequences, and warm-up campaigns all run through email. Your list is the single most valuable asset in an info-product business.

Social presence is the top-of-funnel that feeds the list. Consistent content builds the know/like/trust loop that makes your launch emails land. The pairing works because every piece of social content should have one job: get the right people onto the list.

## Tactic seed (Phase 3 auto-fill)
- Channel #01 → Daily value email + weekly offer email; run an RRO sequence to anyone who opened the last launch email but didn't buy
- Channel #02 → Post one "result or transformation" story per day showing a student outcome or a before/after; end every post with a link to your lead magnet or free training

## Common offer shape
Course creators sell transformation at a fixed price ($97-$2,000) — the offer is framed around a specific outcome the student achieves by the end of the program. Common structures: self-paced course, live cohort with weekly calls, membership with monthly new content, or a hybrid program with group coaching included.

## Dream 100 default
Yes — course creators benefit enormously from borrowed audiences. One podcast appearance or newsletter swap to the right audience can generate hundreds of new subscribers in a single day.

- Blotato or social scheduling connector (for batching and scheduling daily social content)
