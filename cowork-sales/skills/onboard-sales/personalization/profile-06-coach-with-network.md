# Profile 06 — Coach with network

## Who this fits
You're a coach, consultant, or expert who already has meaningful relationships in your industry — former colleagues, event contacts, podcast connections, or a warm network of peers. You know the right people; now you need to activate those relationships systematically. Examples: executive coach with a corporate background, business consultant who speaks at conferences, marketing expert with a podcast and industry relationships.

## Why these channels
Your email list converts your network into an asset you own — people who know you already are dramatically more likely to open your emails, click your links, and buy. A daily or weekly email to even a small warm list (500-2,000 people who already respect you) outperforms a cold email to 100,000 strangers every time.

Borrowed audience is your fastest growth lever — you have the peer relationships to get on podcasts, co-host webinars, and do newsletter swaps. Your network is the asset. The Dream 100 framework formalizes the process of systematically activating those relationships to borrow attention.

## Tactic seed (Phase 3 auto-fill)
- Channel #01 → Send one weekly email with a client story, a framework insight, or a contrarian take; include one CTA to a discovery call or your flagship program
- Channel #07 → This week, do one visible give for one person in your Top 100 (a LinkedIn recommendation, a referral, a resource share); send one collab ask per month

## Common offer shape
Coaches with networks typically sell high-ticket 1:1 or group programs ($2,000-$25,000) framed around a specific transformation — business growth, leadership development, performance improvement. The offer often includes direct access to the coach's time, frameworks, and network.

## Dream 100 default
Yes — this profile is the archetype for the borrowed audience channel. Activating your existing network through a structured give-before-ask system is the primary growth mechanism.
